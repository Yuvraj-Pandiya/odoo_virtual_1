Core Modules

Everything Your Fleet Operations Need

From vehicle registration to trip completion — every workflow automated and rule-enforced.

📊

Live Operations Dashboard

Real-time KPIs: active vehicles, trips in motion, fleet utilization %, and drivers on duty — all in one glance.

🚗

Vehicle Registry & Status

Manage your full fleet with automatic status transitions — Available, On Trip, In Shop, or Retired — enforced by the system.

🧑‍✈️

Driver Management

Track driver profiles, license validity, safety scores, and availability. Expired-license or suspended drivers are auto-blocked.

🗺️

Trip Dispatch & Lifecycle

Create and dispatch trips with source, destination, cargo weight, and auto-validation. Full lifecycle: Draft → Dispatched → Completed → Cancelled.

🔧

Maintenance Tracking

Log maintenance records per vehicle. The system auto-pulls vehicles into "In Shop" status, removing them from the dispatch pool instantly.

⛽

Fuel & Expense Logs

Track fuel consumption, costs, tolls, and operational expenses. Auto-calculates total operational cost per vehicle for reporting.
